F.A.Q

Frequently Asked Questions

  • What types of appliances does Home Assistant repair?

    Home Assistant can repair a wide range of appliances, including AC, TV, Fan, refrigerators, washing machines, dryers, ovens, and more. Simply submit a service request and our team will match you with a qualified service provider.

  • How long does it typically take to complete a repair?

    The time it takes to complete a repair will vary depending on the type of appliance and the nature of the repair. Once a service provider has accepted your request, they will be able to provide an estimated time frame for completing the repair.

  • How do I know if a service provider is qualified to repair my appliance?

    Home Assistant only works with qualified and experienced service providers who have been thoroughly vetted and screened. You can view service provider profiles and reviews on the platform to ensure that you are choosing a qualified provider.

  • What if I'm not satisfied with the service I received?

    If you are not satisfied with the service you received, you can contact our customer support team to discuss your concerns. We will work with you and the service provider to resolve any issues and ensure that you are satisfied with the repair.

  • What payment methods are accepted?

    Home Assistant accepts a variety of payment methods, including credit and debit cards, You can also pay with Bkash, Rocket, Nagad, Upay, and other popular payment platforms. All payments are processed securely through the platform to ensure your privacy and security. If you want you can choose pay cash after the service completes you can do that.

  • Can I track the status of my repair request?

    Yes, Home Assistant provides a service request management system that allows you to track the status of your repair request and communicate with your assigned service provider.

  • Can I cancel a service request?

    Yes, you can cancel a service request at any time before the service provider has accepted the request. Once a service provider has accepted the request, you will need to contact them directly to discuss any cancellations or rescheduling.

  • How do I schedule a service appointment?

    You can schedule a service appointment by creating a service request on the platform. Simply provide the details of the appliance and the issue you are experiencing, and we will match you with a qualified service provider who can complete the repair.

  • How does Home Assistant ensure the privacy and security of my personal information?

    Home Assistant takes data privacy and security very seriously. We use industry-standard encryption, firewalls, and access controls to protect your personal information, and we comply with all relevant data protection regulations.

  • Are Home Assistant's service providers licensed and insured?

    Yes, all of Home Assistant's service providers are fully licensed and insured to perform appliance repairs. We conduct thorough background checks and vetting processes to ensure that our service providers are qualified and trustworthy.

  • Do you offer any guarantees on repairs?

    Yes, we stand behind the quality of our service providers and their repairs. If you experience any issues with a repair, we will work with you and the service provider to resolve the issue and ensure that you are satisfied with the repair.
